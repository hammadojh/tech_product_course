## üéØ 1. Enhanced Feature Backlog

### üéØ Goal:

### ‚úÖ Deliverables:

> - **User Story Writing:** *"User Stories Applied" by Mike Cohn* ‚Äì best practices for writing effective user stories with clear acceptance criteria
> - **Tool:** Consider using product management tools like *ProductPlan, Aha!, or Notion* for roadmap visualization and backlog management
